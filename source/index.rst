Welcome
=======

If you are new to Raspberry Pi and/or Linux and would like some help getting it
installed and working, there are local experts available to help you.

`Ottawa Canada Linux Users' Group`_ (OCLUG_)

.. image:: images/oclug-logo.png
  :target: OCLUG_
  :alt: OCLUG Logo

When
----

| Saturday, May 23, 2015 from 0900h to 1700h (Eastern Time)


Where
-----

| Algonquin College, Woodroffe Campus, Building T, 1st Floor


Cost
----

$0.00.  This is a free event and it is open to anyone and everyone.  If you
wish to make a donation to Algonquin College or to OCLUG, you may do so at the
`Info Desk`_.
